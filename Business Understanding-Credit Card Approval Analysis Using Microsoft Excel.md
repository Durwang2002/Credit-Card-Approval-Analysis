## Step 1: Business Understanding

### Project Title

**Credit Card Approval Analysis Using Microsoft Excel**

### Business Understanding

Banks and financial institutions receive thousands of credit card applications every day. Each applicant has a different financial background, income level, employment status, credit history, and repayment behavior. Approving credit cards without proper analysis can increase the risk of loan defaults, financial losses, and fraudulent activities.

The objective of this project is to analyze customer application data using Microsoft Excel to identify the factors that influence credit card approval decisions. By examining customer demographics, financial information, and application details, the analysis helps uncover patterns associated with approved and rejected applications.

The insights generated from this analysis can help the bank:

* Improve the credit approval process.
* Reduce the risk of approving high-risk applicants.
* Identify characteristics of eligible customers.
* Support data-driven decision-making for credit policies.
* Enhance operational efficiency and customer experience.

### Business Domain

**Banking & Financial Services**

### Stakeholders

* Credit Risk Team
* Credit Card Approval Team
* Risk Analysts
* Business Managers
* Operations Team
* Senior Management

### Expected Business Outcome

Develop an Excel-based analytical solution that enables stakeholders to understand approval trends, monitor key performance indicators, identify risk patterns, and support better credit card approval decisions through interactive analysis and dashboards.
